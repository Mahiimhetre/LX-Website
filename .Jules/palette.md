## 2026-03-31 - Rating Component Accessibility
**Learning:** Custom rating components (like `StarRating`) built with divs and buttons often lack necessary ARIA roles (`radiogroup`, `radio`) and specific focus states for keyboard users, making them unusable for screen readers.
**Action:** Ensure all custom interactive groups use appropriate ARIA roles and maintain strong `focus-visible` states for keyboard navigation.
